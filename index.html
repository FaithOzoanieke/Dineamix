Dineamix Branding

About the Brand

Dine-A-Mix is an exciting Nigerian based restaurant with a strong track record of giving excellent and satisfactory foods and services to our customers. With our experienced and well cultured chefs and staffs we are able to bring pleasure into plates and give you an irreplaceable and luxurious memory. We merge both the organic, vibrant, vivacious and energetic food culture of the Nigerian descent and the 21st century technocratic innovative ways of food services.

The Logo

The logo comprises of a chef mascot and the brand name. Both are custome made. The Mascot stands as a representation of the core of the brand which is that they have excellent chefs that has a humanistic side, which understands both the luxury and expertise required to give maximum pleasure and taste to the customers. The mascot collar is ingrained with a circular like pattern icon which is derived from african symbolism, as a strong message because this brand is rooted in the African culture.
